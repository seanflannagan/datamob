Datamob was a website documenting
public data put to good use.

It highlighted innovative uses of public data, made available by governments and institutions.

It was launched in 2008 by @seanflannagan and @laurensperber and was
directly inspired by Aaron Swartz and his project theinfo.org.

It was Sean and Lauren's first Rails project.

Alas, it is no more.

Check out awesome-public-datasets in its absence. It lists many of the datasets that were featured on Datamob.
